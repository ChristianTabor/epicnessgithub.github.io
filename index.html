The Stratus Network

play.stratus.network

Founded in early 2017, the Stratus Network aims to become the next home for the PGM plugin. With it and other custom-coded plugins, Stratus offers both competitive and casual team-based PvP and, most importantly, a dedicated and awesome community.

Hypixel

mc.hypixel.net

Starting out as a YouTube channel making Minecraft Adventure Maps, Hypixel is now one of the largest and highest quality Minecraft Server Networks in the world, featuring original games such as The Walls, Mega Walls, Blitz Survival Games, and many more!
